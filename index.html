OBJECTIVES

Primary Objective:

To develop a comprehensive Smartphone-based management system that automates and streamlines the daily operations of hardware stores, replacing error-prone manual processes with efficient digital solutions.

Specific Objectives:

  1. Operational Efficiency - Reduce transaction processing time by 70% compared to manual methods, automate inventory tracking with real-time stock updates, and eliminate calculation errors in pricing and totals.
  2. Inventory Control - Maintain accurate stock levels across all product categories, implement automatic reordering alerts for low-stock items, and track product movement patterns.
  3. Business Intelligence - Generate daily, weekly, and monthly sales reports, provide profit analysis and revenue tracking, and create inventory valuation reports.
  4. Customer Service Enhancement - Enable faster checkout processing, maintain customer purchase history, and generate professional receipts and invoices.
  5. Data Security and Management - Secure business data through user authentication, implement regular automated data backups, and maintain comprehensive audit trails of all transactions.

SCOPE AND LIMITATIONS

INCLUDED IN SCOPE

  • Product Management (Add/Edit/Delete products)
  • Inventory Control with real-time tracking
  • Sales Processing (POS interface)
  • Reporting System (Sales, Inventory, P&L)
  • User Management (Role-based access)
  • Customer Management (Database & purchase history)
  • Smartphone-based application
  • Offline operation capability

EXCLUDED FROM SCOPE

  • Online ordering or e-commerce website
  • Barcode scanning integration
  • RFID inventory tracking
  • Supplier portal integration
  • Accounting system integration
  • Multi-branch support
  • Cloud synchronization
  • Real-time online payment processing
  • Rental equipment management
  • Project/quotation management
  • Delivery tracking system

TECHNICAL DETAILS & IMPLEMENTATION

System Architecture:

Platform: Smartphone-based application (Android/iOS)

Database: Local SQLite database for offline operation

User Interface: Responsive mobile interface for different screen sizes

Security: Password authentication with role-based permissions

Implementation Timeline:

Phase 1: Requirements Analysis & Design (2 weeks)

Phase 2: Database & Core Module Development (4 weeks)

Phase 3: Sales & Inventory Module Development (4 weeks)

Phase 4: Reporting & User Management (3 weeks)

Phase 5: Testing & Deployment (3 weeks)

Expected Benefits:

• Reduced manual errors in calculations and inventory tracking

• Faster transaction processing at checkout

• Better inventory control with automatic low-stock alerts

• Improved decision-making with business intelligence reports
