Key Technology

Smartphone-Based Solution: Developed as a mobile application for Android devices, ensuring accessibility and convenience for daily store operations.

Core Purpose

To automate and streamline hardware store operations, replacing manual record-keeping with efficient digital processes that reduce errors and improve efficiency.

PRIMARY OBJECTIVE

System Overview & Goals

Primary Objective

To develop a comprehensive Smartphone-based management system that automates and streamlines the daily operations of hardware stores, replacing error-prone manual processes with efficient digital solutions.

Time Reduction

SPECIFIC OBJECTIVES

Detailed Goals and Targets

1. Operational Efficiency

  • To reduce transaction processing time by 70% compared to manual methods
  • To automate inventory tracking with real-time stock updates
  • To eliminate calculation errors in pricing and totals

2. Inventory Control

  • To maintain accurate stock levels across all product categories
  • To implement automatic reordering alerts for low-stock items
  • To track product movement patterns and identify fast/slow-moving items

3. Business Intelligence

  • To generate daily, weekly, and monthly sales reports
  • To provide profit analysis and revenue tracking
  • To create inventory valuation reports

4. Customer Service Enhancement

  • To enable faster checkout processing
  • To maintain customer purchase history
  • To generate professional receipts and invoices

5. Data Security and Management

  • To secure business data through user authentication
  • To implement regular automated data backups
  • To maintain comprehensive audit trails of all transactions

SCOPE AND LIMITATIONS

Core Management Modules

1. Core Management Modules:

A. PRODUCT MANAGEMENT

  • Add/Edit/Delete products

B. INVENTORY CONTROL

  • Real-time stock level tracking
  • Automatic deduction after sales
  • Manual stock adjustments
  • Stock movement history

C. SALES PROCESSING

  • Point-of-Sale (POS) interface
  • Bill generation with tax calculation
  • Receipt printing
  • Return/Refund processing

D. REPORTING SYSTEM

  • Sales reports (daily, weekly, monthly)
  • Inventory reports
  • Profit/Loss statements
  • Customer purchase history
